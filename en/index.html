You've reached the ocean floor!

Here in Costa Rica, our deepest point plunges to approximately 6000 meters below the surface. But the ultimate deep—the ocean's true floor—is the Challenger Deep in the Western Pacific, descending an astonishing 10,935 meters.

Thank you for taking the plunge into Costa Rica's deep blue.

This journey does not end here, every species you got to know, every zone you explored, is part of an ecosystem that embodies balance, beauty, and the urgent need for conservation. May this journey awaken your curiosity, your respect, and your commitment.

Because protecting life under the sea is, ultimately, protecting what we are.
